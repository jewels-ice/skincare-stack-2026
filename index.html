🧬 Compare the Microneedling Boosters

Microneedling itself creates the channels. What goes through those channels makes the real difference. Here's the honest landscape — most clinics push one or two of these, but they're not all equal.

Booster What it is Source Effect duration vs PDRN
Exosomes (Benev) Cellular signaling vesicles — newest tech Plant or stem-cell derived Pure regenerative signal often better
PRF Platelet-Rich Fibrin — slower spin, no anticoagulants, fibrin matrix Your own blood (autologous) 7–10 days sustained release arguably better
PDRN / Salmon DNA (Rejuran) Purified salmon sperm DNA fragments — Korean gold standard Salmon Activates skin's repair receptors benchmark
PRP Platelet-Rich Plasma — single spin, liquid, older protocol Your own blood (autologous) Hours to 2 days comparable
Peptides Lab-made signaling protein fragments Synthetic Targeted collagen/elastin signal gentler
Stem-Cell Serum Plant-derived stem cell extract (NOT real human stem cells in US) Plants Topical antioxidant signaling weakest
Heads up: "Vampire Facelift" is PRP/PRF microinjections with no microneedling — different treatment, less collagen induction. "Vampire Facial" IS microneedling + PRP/PRF. Confirm by name. And Morpheus8 / RF microneedling is a different machine (needles + radiofrequency heat) — deeper, more downtime. The booster question applies on top of either, but recovery time scales accordingly.

💎 Top Insider Tips

  • Beverly Hills runs a 20–40% zip-code premium for the same modality. Use Marina del Rey / Santa Monica / Westwood / Culver City for everything except the Skin 22 Sofwave promo (which offsets the BH markup).
  • Sciton BBL ≠ generic IPL. If a clinic just says "BBL" or "IPL," confirm the device by name. Skin Laundry uses Q-switched YAG and IPL — not Sciton BBL.
  • Tretinoin via online tele-RX (Apostrophe / Curology / Nurx) is $15–60/month — vastly cheaper than $80+ retinol serums and far more effective.
  • The Ordinary Niacinamide 10% + Zinc (~$8) clinically matches $80 luxury serums.
  • Maelove Glow Maker ($30) is the consistently cited dupe for SkinCeuticals CE Ferulic ($180+).
  • Skip "salmon serum" without microneedling — PDRN molecules can't penetrate intact skin. Real benefit only happens through microneedling channels.
  • Sofwave usually beats Ultherapy on price + comfort + downtime for comparable mid-face results — often $1,000+ cheaper.
  • Don't bundle PDRN + exosomes + cold plasma in one session. Marginal evidence beyond PDRN alone — pay for one well-done modality.
  • BBL is best in fall / winter — sun avoidance window is easier to hold. Many West LA clinics run "BBL season" specials Oct–Feb.

🚫 What's NOT in the stack (hard exclusions)

  • Botox / Dysport / Xeomin / Daxxify (any neurotoxin injectables)
  • Dermal fillers (HA, Sculptra, Radiesse, fat transfer)
  • Cosmetic surgery (facelift, blepharoplasty, surgical thread lifts)
  • Unregulated supplements with no published clinical evidence
